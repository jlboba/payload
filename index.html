GENJI

  • ACCURACY: 0.7
  • DEFENSE: 0.3
  • ATTACK MOVE: Genji quickly throws shurikens three at a time
  • DEFENSE MOVE: With his katana, Genji can deflect oncoming damage by swiping the sword quickly

STORY

  • The youngest son in the Shimada clan, Genji enjoyed being a ninja but had no interest in the family's (illegal) business. When his older brother confronted him to take more interest in the family empire, things got violent and Genji died — almost. He was rescued by Overwatch, who rebuilt his injured body into what he is today, a cyborg ninja.

PHARAH

  • ACCURACY: 0.7
  • DEFENSE: 0.3
  • ATTACK MOVE: Pharah uses a rocket launcher to, well, launch rockets
  • DEFENSE MOVE: Covered in combat armor, Pharah has jump jets that allow her to fly in the air

STORY

  • Hailing from a long line of soldiers that served with honor, Pharah always dreamed of becoming a part of Overwatch — and that, she did. However, after Overwatch disbanded the first time around, Pharah found herself working as a guard for a private security firm. Now, though, with Overwatch piecing back together, there's nothing she loves more than to fight to make a difference.

— DEFENDERS

BASTION

  • ACCURACY: 0.5
  • DEFENSE: 0.5
  • ATTACK MOVE: In his stationary mode, Bastion attacks with a rapid gattling gun
  • DEFENSE MOVE: As a robot, Bastion can use his programmed self-repair to repair himself when damaged

STORY

  • Bastion was one of many factory made robots originally meant to be peacekeepers. But when war started waging between humans and robots, all Bastions were programmed to kill humans. After the war ended and all Bastions were destroyed, this particular Bastion was forgotten in the forest. After being reactivated accidentally, Bastion has become a generally peaceful robot simply interested in nature.

MEI

  • ACCURACY: 0.5
  • DEFENSE: 0.5
  • ATTACK MOVE: With her endothermic blaster, Mei sprays a stream of frost that slowly freezes and damages her target
  • DEFENSE MOVE: With her weather-altering device, Mei can surround herself in an ice block that protects her from damage

STORY

  • A previous scientist of Overwatch's initiative to find the solution to global warming, Mei was sent to monitor Antartica. While there, a sudden polar storm left her and her fellow scientists stranded, and when their supplies ran low they entered cryostasis to survive. Rescue didn't come until years later, and Mei was the only one to survive. Now, she still fights to save the environment by traveling the world and continuing her research.
